自由飞鸟音乐播放器
freebird music player

一个本地音乐播放器，有一些简单的功能，支持歌词显示
A native music player that has some simple features and supports lyrics display

使用说明 instruction：
	界面下方按钮控制播放，或使用空格键
	界面右方按钮控制音量和循环
	歌词用鼠标滚轮上下滑动，或使用h和n键
	单击顶部的歌曲文件名在歌词列表,歌曲列表和歌曲信息中切换
	按下b键可切换背景，歌词高亮显示颜色也会变
	单击右上方的exit退出程序
	
	The bottom button controls the play, or USES the space key
	The right side of the interface controls the volume and circulation
	The lyrics are sliding down the mouse wheel, or using h and n keys
	Click on the top of the song name in the lyrics list, the song list and the song infomation
	Press b key to switch background, the lyrics are bright and the color will change
	Click the 'exit' exit program at the top right
	
	注：
	编辑歌曲简介需要在文件名后加上" $$ "再输入简介！不能换行！结尾再加一个空格！
